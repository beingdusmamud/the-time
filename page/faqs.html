Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our academic portal and services.

General Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account:

  1. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
  2. Fill in your academic email address
  3. Complete the registration form
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Log in to access all features

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for reset instructions
  4. Click the reset link and create a new password

What are the system requirements?

Minimum system requirements:

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • JavaScript enabled
  • Stable internet connection
  • Screen resolution: 1280x720 or higher
  • PDF viewer for documents

Academic Questions

How do I access course materials?

To access course materials:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Navigate to "My Courses"
  3. Select the desired course
  4. Click on "Course Materials" tab
  5. Download or view materials online

Technical Questions

Why won't my files upload?

Common upload issues and solutions:

  • Check file size (max 50MB)
  • Ensure supported file format
  • Check internet connection
  • Clear browser cache
  • Try a different browser
