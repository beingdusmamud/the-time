Frequently Asked Questions

How to download Previous Year Question Papers?

Simply click on the "Download PDF" button on any question paper card. The PDF will either open in a new tab or start downloading automatically, depending on your browser settings.

Are all semester papers available?

Yes, we provide question papers for all six semesters of Electrical Engineering. Use the semester filter to find papers for your specific semester.

How often are new papers added?

New papers are added within 1-2 weeks after each examination. We ensure to provide both mid-term and end-term examination papers.

Why can't I find a specific year's paper?

While we strive to provide all papers, some might be unavailable due to various reasons. If you're looking for a specific paper, please contact us, and we'll try to help you find it.

Are solution keys available with the question papers?

Currently, we provide only question papers. Solution keys or answer sheets are not available due to university policies. However, you can consult with your faculty for solutions.

What file format are the papers in?

All papers are provided in PDF format for easy viewing and printing. Make sure you have a PDF reader installed on your device.
