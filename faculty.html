About Faculty Dashboard

Purpose

A comprehensive tool designed to help faculty members track their teaching schedules, manage classes, and stay organized throughout the academic semester.

Features

  • Real-time schedule tracking
  • Current & upcoming class alerts
  • Multi-semester view
  • Working day management
  • Holiday notifications

How to Use

Simply locate your name card to view your current schedule. Toggle between semesters using the semester tabs. Current and upcoming classes are highlighted automatically.

Disclaimer

This dashboard is intended for informational purposes only. While we strive to maintain accurate and up-to-date information, schedules may be subject to change. Faculty members should:

  • Verify schedules with official department communications
  • Check for any last-minute changes or announcements
  • Report any discrepancies to the department administration

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find my current class schedule?

Your current schedule is displayed on your personalized teacher card. The card shows:

  • Current/upcoming class with real-time status
  • Complete schedule for both 4th and 6th semesters
  • Room assignments and timing details
What do the different class status indicators mean?
In Progress

Currently ongoing class

Upcoming

Next scheduled class

Completed

Previous classes

How often is the schedule updated?

The dashboard updates automatically:

  • Real-time updates for current class status
  • Every 5 minutes for schedule changes
  • Immediate updates for holiday announcements
What should I do if I notice a scheduling error?

If you notice any discrepancies in your schedule:

  1. Document the specific error (time, room, subject)
  2. Contact your department administrator
  3. Continue following the current schedule until officially notified of changes
How do I switch between semester views?

Use the semester tabs in your teacher card to switch views:

  • Click "4th Semester" for fourth semester schedule
  • Click "6th Semester" for sixth semester schedule
  • Both views maintain real-time status updates
How are holidays and working days determined?

The working day system follows the official academic calendar:

  • Regular working days are numbered sequentially
  • Holidays are marked with special notices
  • Working day count resets each semester
